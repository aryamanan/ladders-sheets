Link your LeetCode account

Shows a read-only "solved on LeetCode" mark next to matching items -- it never checks/unchecks your own progress, that's always manual. LeetCode has no public CORS-friendly API, so this goes through a third-party community proxy (alfa-leetcode-api) and may be unreliable or occasionally down (free-tier host, can take ~30s to wake up on the first request).

Progress saves automatically to this browser, and to your account when signed in.
