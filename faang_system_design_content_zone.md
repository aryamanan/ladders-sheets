# System Design Content Zone

An exhaustive topic index across the major system design prep platforms -- fundamentals, key technologies, interview patterns, and case studies -- organized into one curriculum instead of five separate course outlines. Every linked title points to a genuinely free page (compiled by hand, not scraped): ByteByteGo's free course chapters, Design Gurus' free crash-course docs, Educative's free preview lessons from Grokking the System Design Interview, Hello Interview's free guide, AlgoMaster's free system design track, the System Design Primer (fully open, CC BY 4.0), and karanpratapsingh/system-design as a general reference. Topics with no verified free link yet are marked `(yet to be linked)` -- bring a URL and it gets slotted in.

Style matches the Extreme Mastery sheets: broad topic buckets, no Foundation/Reinforcement/Boss ladders, `{Platform}` tag per item.
